I am a researcher in the fields of haptic science and haptic engineering, focusing on the exploration of skin biomechanics to decode and replicate our sense of touch. I am the founder of SmartHap LLC., where we leverage the capabilities of smart materials and smart structures to create cutting-edge multimodal sensing and actuating platforms. Our mission is to revolutionize the communication and interaction between the users and various systems, elevating the overall tactile experiences.

I received my M.S and Ph.D in Systems Engineering at the University of Virgina in 2020 and 2023, respectively, where I worked in Gerling Touch Lab advised by Prof. Gerling.

Research interest

  • Fundemental research in haptic/tactile perception

  • Development of electromechanical devices for enhancing human-centered systems

  • Interested domain includes but not limited to: soft robotics, telecommunication, biomedical devices and human-robot interaction (HRI)
